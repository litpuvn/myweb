Welcome

Welcome to the home page of AI Enabled Healthcare Lab at the School of Applied Computational Sciences (SACS), Meharry Medical College. Welcome to join us!

News: We have research openings for PhD., Master, and Undergraduate students. Here, you will have great experience to lean cutting-edge Machine Learning and Data Mining skills with applications in healthcare.

Research Interest

Our broad research interest is in data mining and machine learning with application in healthcare setting. Some specific areas are including but not limited to computer vision, natural language processing, time-series analysis and anomaly detection. Potential applications are developing models and algorithms that incorporate community knowledge for strategic planning in the health care setting, using mobile health technology for the early detection and monitoring of patient heath.
